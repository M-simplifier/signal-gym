# Status

Signal Gym is an MVP prototype.

Implemented:

- PureScript/Asterism SPA
- Three playable training drills
- Expanded synthetic exercise corpus with local session randomization
- Adaptive per-drill levels
- Local progress persistence
- Static build for GitHub Pages
- Public-safety audit notes

Not implemented:

- Clinical validation
- Account sync
- Telemetry
- User-imported text
- Baseline or longitudinal transfer studies

Public claim boundary:

- Allowed: evidence-informed, local-first training prototype for attention, working memory, and dense-reading practice.
- Not allowed: medical treatment, ADHD intervention, IQ improvement, guaranteed productivity improvement, or proven far transfer.
